You're running the free → discounted → full price model. This is smart **IF you execute it deliberately.**

- Possibly 1 more (maybe someone from Southern Legends network)

**Phase 2: DEEP DISCOUNT (2-3 projects, ~2 months)**
- Pricing: $1,500-$2,000 per build
- Frame as: "Early adopter pricing" or "portfolio acceleration rate"
- **Goal:** Test if people will pay, start generating some revenue

**Phase 3: MODERATE DISCOUNT (2-3 projects, ~2 months)**
- Pricing: $3,000-$4,000 per build
- **Goal:** Validate market fit at higher price point

**Phase 4: FULL PRICE & ABOVE**
- Pricing: $5,000-$8,000+ per build
- **Goal:** Establish as premium offering

### Critical Rules for Flywheel Success
1. **Explicit communication:** When you deliver free work, say: "Next project is $X"
2. **Real timeline:** Not perpetual free — you have a defined endpoint
3. **Quality non-negotiable:** Each phase must deliver visible results
4. **Move UP:** Don't stay at free or discount indefinitely
