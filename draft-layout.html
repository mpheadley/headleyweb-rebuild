Ken Burns Effect Intensity

Slow zoom/pan on hero and photo-break sections. Watch each box — the image is continuously animating. Which speed feels right?

Why Ken Burns over parallax: Works perfectly on mobile (parallax breaks on iOS), more forgiving of lower-quality photos (motion draws the eye), and doesn't conflict with angled section dividers.
A — Subtle (5% zoom)

Your Hero Headline

Barely perceptible. Adds life without being distracting. Best for professional/serious tone.

B — Medium (12% zoom + pan)

Your Hero Headline

Noticeable but not distracting. Cinematic feel. The sweet spot for most sites.

C — Dramatic (25% zoom + pan)

Your Hero Headline

Very visible motion. Bold and cinematic. Can feel restless on longer page visits. Best for landing pages with short dwell time.

Recommendation

Option A (subtle) for the homepage hero — visitors spend time here and heavy motion gets distracting. Option B (medium) for photo-break sections between content blocks where the motion draws the eye as you scroll past.
