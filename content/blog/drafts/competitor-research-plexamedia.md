- Established local client relationships
- Broad service offering (web + print + video)

## Weaknesses / Our Opportunities

1. **No team transparency** — no bios, no faces, no personal story on the site
2. **AI messaging is tool-focused, not outcome-focused** — reads like a SaaS pitch, not a service business
3. **WordPress only** — bloated themes, slower PageSpeed, no modern framework
4. **No messaging framework** — copy talks about what they do, not what the customer gets
5. **Generalist positioning** — serves everyone (businesses + ministries + nationwide), no niche focus
6. **No visible pricing** — standard agency opacity
