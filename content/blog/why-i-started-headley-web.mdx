You're great at what you do. Google just doesn't know it yet.

When I was in pastoral ministry and running our family flower farm, I knew we had so much to offer — but I had no idea how to get the word out. I was too busy in the trenches to spend time worrying about Google. **A lot of small business owners around here feel the same way.**

## The Realization

After closing that chapter, I realized something. The small business owners who keep this community running are incredible at what they do, but most of them are invisible online. And the ones who do have a website? They're frustrated that it's not working for them the way it should.

I kept seeing the same pattern: a business owner pours money into a website that looks fine on the surface but doesn't generate a single lead. The phone doesn't ring. The contact form collects dust. And they start to wonder if having a website even matters.

It does. It just has to be built the right way.

## What I'm Building

That's why I launched Headley Web & SEO.

I've spent the last several months building websites and marketing systems for small businesses in Northeast Alabama. My goal isn't to build "pretty websites." It's to build **clear, simple sites that actually get found on Google** — so you can get back to doing the work you love.

Every site I build follows the [StoryBrand framework](/blog/why-your-website-isnt-converting), which means your customer's problem is front and center, the path forward is obvious, and there's no guessing about what to do next.

## Who This Is For

If you're a contractor, tradesperson, or local service provider who knows your work speaks for itself — but your website doesn't — that's exactly who I built this for.

You shouldn't have to become a marketing expert to get found online. You just need someone in your corner who understands the tools and can make them work for you.
