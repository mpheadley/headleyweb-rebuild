## The Three Channels (and How They Work Together)

| Channel | Purpose | Audience | Tone |
|---------|---------|----------|------|
| **Matt's personal profile** | Highest-reach channel. Re-entry point. Human trust. | Friends, family, JSU alumni, local acquaintances | Personal, honest, short |
| **Southern Legends Page** | Community content. Discovery engine. Name recognition. | NE Alabama locals, community group members | Editorial, place-based, no CTAs |
| **Headley Web & SEO Page** | Business credibility. Converts trust into clients. | Business owners, referral sources | Professional, proof-based, has CTAs |

**The flow:** Personal profile builds warmth and reach → SL builds local authority and name recognition → HW converts that into business. They feed each other but stay distinct.

## 1. Matt's Personal Profile

### Re-Entry Plan (Starting from Months of Silence)

The goal is to get back to posting naturally without making the gap a big deal. Nobody tracks how long you've been quiet. They just see a post in their feed.

**Sequence:**
1. **Heather's 40th birthday post** — natural, personal, gets easy engagement (likes, happy birthdays). Warms up the profile. Do this first.
2. **SL share (1-2 days later)** — share the SL page's Noccalula Falls post. Weave in one line about the hard season, don't make it a whole thing. Tag Heather.
   - Example: "Been a rough couple years. Started writing about people and places around here as a way back in. First one's Noccalula Falls — where I proposed to Heather 17 years ago."
3. **Normal posting resumes** — no schedule, no pressure. Post when something's worth sharing.

**Rules:**
- Don't do a standalone "re-intro" or "I'm back" post. It puts too much weight on the gap. Let the content be the re-entry.
- Don't explain the hard season in detail. One line is enough. People who know will read between the lines.
- Don't apologize for being gone. Nobody was counting.

### Ongoing Role
- **Reshare SL posts** from the Page — this is how you tag people (Heather, friends, subjects) since Pages can only tag other Pages
- **Reshare HW posts** when relevant (client launches, milestones)
- **Participate in local Facebook groups** as yourself — answer questions, be helpful, don't pitch
- **Personal posts** when you feel like it — no obligation, no schedule

### What NOT to Do From Personal Profile
- Don't pitch Headley Web services in personal posts
- Don't turn your personal profile into a business feed
- Don't share every SL profile — pick the ones with personal hooks or strong local interest

### Content Rhythm
- Post with every new profile (no fixed schedule — publish-driven)
- Per-post checklist: native photos (faces first), link in first comment, check in, tag subject's page, reshare from personal profile

### Growth Triggers
| Milestone | Action |
|-----------|--------|

| 5 published profiles | Share the site itself in Facebook groups |
| 10 profiles | Pitch local media (Anniston Star, Jacksonville News, JSU student paper) |
| Visible social engagement (200-300+ followers) | Launch Patreon |

## 4. Facebook Groups Strategy

### How to Use Groups
- **Join as Matt (personal profile)**, not as either Page
- **Lurk for a week** before participating in any new group
- **Be helpful first** — answer questions about websites, Google, SEO without linking to anything
- **Link to your Page or site only when someone asks** ("who does websites around here?")
- **Share SL profiles in groups** with custom intros tailored to each group's location/interest

### Groups to Join
- Calhoun County community groups
- NE Alabama groups
- Jacksonville groups
- JSU alumni groups
- Gadsden / Etowah County groups
- Local buy/sell/trade (sparingly, when relevant)
- Church/youth leader networks
- Local business owner groups

### Rules
- Don't join a group and immediately post a link
- Don't copy-paste the same intro across groups
- Don't post more than one SL profile per group per week
- If a group has rules about self-promotion, follow them

## 6. Future Platforms

### Instagram (Add When Ready)
- **For Southern Legends only** — typographic cards, place photos, behind-the-scenes
- **Trigger:** After SL has 5+ profiles and Facebook is running smoothly
- **Format:** Feed posts for profiles, Stories for quick reach, Reels only if natural video exists
- **Link in bio** to latest profile
- Don't spread yourself thin — Facebook first, Instagram second

### TikTok
- Skip. Not where the NE Alabama audience is right now.

### LinkedIn
- Optional for Headley Web — not a priority for local service business clients
- Could help for B2B leads (corporate team building, larger businesses)
- Don't create until you have bandwidth

### Google Business Profile (Posts)
- **Headley Web:** Post updates when you have client wins or blog posts
- **Southern Legends:** Only if SL gets its own GBP (not urgent)

---

## 7. What to Skip

- **Paid ads** — organic local group reach is still high. Ads are for when organic plateaus.
- **Content calendar spreadsheet** — SL's per-profile distribution checklist IS the calendar. HW posts 2-4x/month, no fixed dates needed.
- **Scheduling tools** (Buffer, Hootsuite, etc.) — overkill at this volume. Post manually.
- **Hashtags** — low impact on Facebook. Skip them.
